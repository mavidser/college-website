Computer Graphics & Multimedia Lab




Computer Graphics Lab

Objective is to make students aware of the concepts underlying modern Computer Graphics and Machine Vision. At the end of the course the student will have the generic skills to design algorithms for digital image synthesis for a broad-based set of computing problems in various domains. This course contains 2D geometric transformations , Algorithms for clipping, 3D geometric and modeling transformation, Illumination models and surface rendering methods etc.

Multimedia Lab

The objective of the laboratory is to support research and education of Computer Science students in the fields of Multimedia Technology such as image editing, photo-retouching, web design, etc.

The laboratory currently has 36 PC workstations with a variety of software. Other resources of the laboratory including Digital Camera, Web Cam, Head Phone, Flat Bed Scanner, Printer etc.

Currently following Multimedia Software is in use in this lab:

  • Adobe Photoshop CS
  • Adobe Premier 6.5
  • Adobe Illustrator CS
  • Flash MX 2004 MVLP with Media
  • Macromedia Dreamweaver MX 2004
  • Director MX
  • Windows Live Movie Maker
  • Animator
